Active Recall

Flashcards allow users to use active recall to study for different topics. Active recall is when you actively stimulate your memory and remember a certain piece of information. This method allows the user to connect the information that is stored int heir long-term memory. This method is also believed to be the most efficient way to study and has proven to be extremely beneficial.
